Program Overview

Nurturing young minds through exploration and discovery

Our Pre-Primary School program provides a nurturing environment where children develop foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and scientific inquiry. We focus on holistic development through a balanced curriculum that includes:

  • Project-based learning activities
  • Integrated arts and music education
  • Social-emotional learning curriculum
  • Outdoor education and nature exploration
  • Technology integration with 1:1 iPad program
